About Us

The Global Tee Sax Innovation is a music education, talent development, and social impact organization dedicated to nurturing young people through quality music training, mentorship, and character development. Since its establishment in 2024, we have trained hundreds of students across schools and communities in Nigeria and internationally, helping them discover their potential, build confidence, and develop essential life skills. Our training include: Piano, Saxophone, Guitar, Violin, Drum-set Talking-Drum and Vocal training. Our training covered both physical and online, flexible for any part of the world.
